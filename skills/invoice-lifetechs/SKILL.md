# Invoice Lifetechs

Automates monthly invoice generation for Lifetechs (株式会社ライフテックス) client.

## Workflow

### 1. Navigate to Freee

1. Open Freee invoice system: https://invoice.secure.freee.co.jp/
2. Ensure you're logged into **THE MOLTS account** (check top-right corner)
3. Navigate to 請求書 (Invoices) section

### 2. Duplicate Previous Month's Invoice

1. Search for most recent Lifetechs invoice (use search or filter by client name)
2. Open the invoice
3. Click the "送付" dropdown → select "入金待ち" or similar option to duplicate
4. Confirm duplication

### 3. Update Invoice Details

Update the following fields for the current month:

- **請求日** (Invoice Date): Last day of current month (e.g., 2026-01-31 for January)
- **入金期日** (Payment Due Date): Last day of following month (e.g., 2026-02-28 for January invoice)
- **件名** (Subject): `YYYY年MM月度ご請求書` (e.g., "2026年1月度ご請求書")
- **品目** (Item Description): `YYYY年MM月月次コンサルティング` (e.g., "2026年1月月次コンサルティング")
- **金額** (Amount): Verify from spreadsheet (typically 220,000円 including tax for Lifetechs)

**Important notes:**
- Use triple-click to select date fields, then type the date directly
- Double-check all dates are correct before saving
- The invoice number will be auto-generated by Freee

### 4. Save Invoice

1. Click 保存 (Save) button
2. Note the invoice number (e.g., #0409)
3. Wait for save confirmation

### 5. Download PDF

1. Click 送付 dropdown → select "PDFダウンロード"
2. PDF will download to Downloads folder
3. Filename format: `株式会社ライフテックス御中_YYYY年MM月度ご請求書_請求書_####.pdf`

### 6. Upload to Google Drive

1. Navigate to Google Drive folder structure:
   - Path: `共有アイテム > THE MOLTS > 永田 > YYYY年 > MM月 > THE MOLTS > 永田`
   - Create folders if they don't exist
2. Manually drag and drop the PDF from Downloads to the folder
3. Verify upload completion

### 7. Record Invoice Number in Spreadsheet

1. Open spreadsheet: https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1d13WY_amm2ehWCCfqzLt5kJrjR35oS03jGYD9wdPqm4/edit
2. Go to 永田 さおり tab
3. Find row 15 (THE MOLT - ライフテックス - 松田)
4. Locate the current month's column (search for "MM月" header in row 1)
5. In the 請求No column for that month (check row 14 header), enter the invoice number without the # (e.g., "409")
6. Verify the entry is saved

## Client Details

- **Company**: 株式会社ライフテックス
- **Contact**: 松田 (Matsuda)
- **商流** (Distribution Channel): THE MOLT
- **Freee Account**: THE MOLTS
- **Typical Amount**: 200,000円 (pre-tax), 220,000円 (with 10% tax)
- **Spreadsheet Row**: 15

## Notes

- This workflow is for monthly recurring invoices
- Each month requires separate execution (January invoice ≠ February invoice)
- PDF upload to Google Drive is currently a manual step
- Slack notification to client (松田雄一) is handled separately outside this workflow
